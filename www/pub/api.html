API

A brief overview of the options that are supported by Bobbograph.

Basic Usage

Bobbograph can be instantiated one of two ways:

var graph = new Bobbograph( element, options );

or...

var graph = Bobbograph.init( element, options );

Options

This is the core object that is passed into Bobbograph.

The following options are available:

PropertyTypeDescription
widthNumberThe width of the container will be set to this value.
heightNumberThe height of the container will be set to this value.
dataData OptionsCustomize the way that your data is parsed.
paddingPadding OptionsCustomize the padding surrounding your graph.
lineLine OptionsCustomize the visual details of the line.
fillFill OptionsCustomize the fill displayed under the area of the line.
frameFrame OptionsCustomize the framing of the graph.
animationAnimation OptionsCustomize the intro animation of your graph.
xAxisAxis OptionsCustomize the x-axis.
yAxisAxis OptionsCustomize the y-axis.
bevelBevel OptionsCustomize the bevel effect applied to your graph.
shadowShadow OptionsCustomize the drop-shadow applied to your graph.

Data

PropertyTypeDescription
vertexBooleanWhen enabled, this will remove all points between vertexes, creating a smoother graph.
maxPointsNumberYour data will be reduced to fit within a maximum number of points.
normalizeBooleanNot yet available.

Padding

PropertyTypeDescription
sizeNumberWill be used for all sides unless overridden.
xNumberWill be used for both left and right values unless overridden.
yNumberWill be used for both top and bottom values unless overridden.
leftNumberPadding to the left of your graph.
rightNumberPadding to the right of your graph.
topNumberPadding to the top of your graph.
bottomNumberPadding to the bottom of your graph.

Line

PropertyTypeDescription
fillFill OptionsThe fill to be applied to the line.
widthNumberThe thickness of the line.
smoothBooleanWhen enabled, the line will be smoothed using quadratic curves.

Fill

PropertyTypeDescription
colorStringA color to be applied to your line.
gradientArrayAn array of colors to be applied to your line.
verticalBooleanWhen enabled, the gradient will be vertical.

Frame

PropertyTypeDescription
xmaxNumberThe maximum x-value to be displayed in the graph.
xminNumberThe minimum x-value to be displayed in the graph.
ymaxNumberThe maximum y-value to be displayed in the graph.
yminNumberThe minimum y-value to be displayed in the graph.

Animation

PropertyTypeDescription
durationNumberThe duration (in milliseconds) of the intro animation.
callbackFunctionA function to be called after the animation is completed.

Axis

PropertyTypeDescription
incrementNumberThe distance between axis lines.

Bevel

PropertyTypeDescription
shineNumberThe intensity of the shine applied to your line.
shadowNumberThe intensity of the shadow applied to your line.
smoothBooleanWhen enabled, the bevel effect will be smoothed.
opacityNumberThe overall opacity of the bevel effect.

Shadow

PropertyTypeDescription
xNumberThe x-offset of the shadow.
yNumberThe y-offset of the shadow.
colorStringThe color of the drop-shadow.
