What is Bobbograph?

Bobbograph is a graphing library built around the idea of providing maximum visual customization.

Why choose Bobbograph?

Customization

Bobbograph gives you the ability to customize just about any aspect of your graph, giving you complete control.

Compatibility

Bobbograph uses best practices in Canvas to ensure that your graphs will look perfect in any browser.

Ease-of-use

Bobbograph is specifically built to be as intuitive as possible.

Help support Bobbograph.

Bobbograph is hosted on Github. Please feel free to contribute to the project, or log issues there.
